The artist

DjJurlan Remix

"DjJurlan" is a Filipino DJ spinner and producer who has been producing music remixes for over 4 years. Most of his remixes are of those songs that are trending on Tiktok and other viral videos that can be made for a remix. He is known all over the Philippines as one of the best in terms of making tiktok dance remixes, breaklatin remixes, reggaeton remixes, bomb/budots remixes, and reggae remixes. In terms of original music, DJ Jurlan also made original music and remixes, that is, he owned it 100%. And that original music is all uploaded to his YT channel also (if you don't know, some of this becomes trending on tiktok and other social media platforms and receives millions of views and thousands of reactions). You can check it out here.

To finish it, thank you for visiting this website. DjJurlan Remix is very thankful that you are here to listen to his music creations and remixes. Explore, enjoy, and Godbless.
